About Us

Ensuring Digital Truth and Integrity.

The Digital Evidence Integrity Validator is a state-of-the-art platform designed to protect the sanctity of digital evidence in the modern age. Our mission is to provide law enforcement, legal professionals, and organizations with the tools they need to ensure that digital files remain untampered and verifiable.

Using advanced hashing algorithms and secure monitoring systems, we create a foolproof digital paper trail. Our system is built on the core principles of security, transparency, and accountability.

Why Digital Evidence Integrity Matters?

  • 🛡️ Blockchain-Inspired Hashing: Every file uploaded is instantly processed using SHA-256 hashing to create a unique digital fingerprint, ensuring any modification is immediately detectable.
  • 👤 Biometric Authorization: Critical modifications require real-time administrative face recognition to prevent unauthorized access or accidental tampering.
  • 👁️ Active Session Monitoring: Our system detects suspicious behavior like window switching or tab changes during sensitive operations to maintain a secure environment.
  • 📜 Immutable Audit Logs: Every action—from login to file modification—is recorded in a permanent, unchangeable log for complete accountability and transparency.
